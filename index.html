My Background

Welcome to my website! My name is Abiral Timalsina, and I am a cybersecurity student training at the Mindrisers Institute in Putalisadak, Kathmandu, Nepal.

This is my very first project of this kind! I built this site to serve as my personal learning log and portfolio. My Learning Goals

The four primary areas I am focusing on during my training

⚡

Ethical Hacking

I am studying how security vulnerabilities are identified and analyzed. By understanding the techniques used to scan systems and identify open vectors, I learn how to build stronger, more resilient defenses.

🔍

SOC Operations

Security Operations Centers (SOC) form the front lines of defense. I am learning how to monitor logs, identify anomalous behavior, analyze network patterns, and coordinate response strategies.

🌐

Web Security

Web applications are highly targeted assets. I focus on studying vulnerabilities like the OWASP Top 10 (SQL injection, cross-site scripting) and exploring defensive mechanisms like Web Application Firewalls (WAF).

🐧

Linux Systems

Linux runs the majority of internet servers and security auditing distributions. I am training in command-line operations, permission management, file audits, and automation scripting.

Career Aspirations

My aim is to establish a career as a Security Analyst or penetration tester, helping institutions in Nepal audit their networks and secure their user data.

Upon finishing my training at Mindrisers, I plan to focus on gaining practical certifications, including eJPT (eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester) and CompTIA Security+, and eventually work towards the OSCP (Offensive Security Certified Professional) to build and validate hands-on security skills.
